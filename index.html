Re-Cycle

Join us in Re-Cycling!

Re-Cycling is possible for all people of all ages and backgrounds! We offer trikes, electric bicycles and regular bicycles! Wether you haven't ridden a bike since your childhood or suit up every weekend and bike for miles upon miles, we offer a unique way to sightsee cities like never before.

Re-Cycle benefits both you and the enviroment!

Re-Cycle has a mission. We want to become the leading method for tourists (or curious locals!) to experience the beauty and flexibility of cities and their back-alleys in an enviromentally concious way! This is also a perfect way to exercise both the body and the mind!

Where to find us?

Re-Cycle is currently just in Norway at all the bigger cities: Oslo, Trondheim, Bergen and Stavanger! We have one location in all of these cities which functions as a office and pick-up zone for the bikes. Feel free to stop by for a friendly chat if you want!
